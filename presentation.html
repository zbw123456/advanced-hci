Limitations & Future Directions

‚ö†Ô∏è Current Limitations

  • Model Accuracy: Demo CNN is untrained (random predictions)
  • Single Modality: Only camera input is functional
  • UI: Console-based, no graphical interface
  • Persistence: In-memory only, no long-term storage
  • FER Accuracy: Real-world ~70% vs lab benchmarks

üöÄ Future Enhancements

  • Contextual AI: Integrate calendar/activity data
